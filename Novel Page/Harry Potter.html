Harry Potter

Book description:

Harry, a child who survived alone after losing his parents to Voldemort, the dark devil, spends his unhappy childhood raised by Aunt Muggle Petunia and Aunt Vernon, who are not wizards, for safety. On his 11th birthday, Harry, who learned through Hagrid that he was a wizard, entered Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and began a tremendous adventure with his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley to find the eternal wizard's stone.
