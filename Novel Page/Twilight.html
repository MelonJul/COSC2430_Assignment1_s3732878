Book description:

"Twilight" series of suspense romance depicting the love between vampires and humans. It is a very popular novel that has become a bestseller on the New York Times and Amazon, and it sensibly depicts the love between a vampire boy and a human girl by thrillingly changing the theme of unhappy lovers. Bella, who moved to Fawkes, meets Edward, a beautiful and mysterious boy. But somehow, from the first meeting, Edward reveals outright hostility, and before long Bella learns the shocking secret that Edward and his family are vampires
