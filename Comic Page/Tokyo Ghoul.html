Tokyo Ghoul

Book description:

It delicately describes the character's psychological state and closely intertwined human relationships, and features a cruel and intense development. It contains many thriller and mystery elements, maintaining a gloomy atmosphere throughout the work. The first part feels like a horror movie, but as the story progresses, it is mainly composed of the battle of the competent person and the tragic story of the main character. Specifically, the theme of "the agony of the main character between man and the goul" is consistently maintained. Although there are many battle scenes, the story of "the confrontation between the roe and the human" is the top priority. Rather than leading to a specific group or a specific person, the development method unfolds in a way that the story alternates with the eyes of both Guul and CCG (human) and meets in one event.
